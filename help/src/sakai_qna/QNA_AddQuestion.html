Add a question

Note: this feature is only applicable for those with update rights

If you wish to add questions to the Q&A tool:

  • Click 'Add a question'
  • Enter your question text in the field provided
  • Choose whether you'd like to be notified any answers to your question
  • Assign your question to an existing category or create a new category
  • Add any attachments
  • Click 'Add question'
